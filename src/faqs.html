03
Why is a session time different at the Cineplex than what is shown in the newspaper or internet movie listings?

We are required to submit our session times for newspaper and internet listings at least 3 days in advance and even earlier when there is a public holiday. Hence, when there are changes to the schedules, these cannot be corrected as amendments are not accepted after submission. To confirm a session time, please check the updated schedules on our website or at the box office.

04
How many days in advance can I book tickets?

Once session is available.

05
What time does your box office open and close?

The box office will open at 10.00am and will close half an hour after the last showtime in the evening. The opening and closing hours of our box office varies as they depend on first movie and the last movie of the day at the cineplex. Furthermore, Reel Cinemas may offer midnight screenings, and double midnight screenings, hence the variations of timing.

Can I cancel my booking and request for a refund?

After your booking is confirmed and paid for, no cancellations or refunds will be allowed under any circumstances. All tickets sold are non-returnable and non-refundable, even if you do not turn up to collect your tickets.

08
How can I make changes to my booking?

You can only make changes to your booking by going directly to the box office of the Cineplex where you booked your tickets. This is subject to seat availability for the movie and/or showtime that you want to change to. You need to make the changes at the box office at least 2 hours before the movie showtime that you originally booked. No changes can be made after the movie that you originally booked has started and all tickets and bookings fees are non-refundable.

2) Any recording devices are strictly prohibited inside the cinema

3) Reel Cinemas strictly adheres to the film rating regulations of the UAE and reserves the right to refuse admission if proof of age cannot be given.

Movies 3d

  • What can I expect when watching a 3D movie?
  • Natus eaque doloribus optio labore dolore ab quis?
01
What can I expect when watching a 3D movie?

The visual presentation system employed in 3D movies attempts to maintain or recreate moving images of the third dimension, giving the illusion of depth as seen by the patron. So patrons can expect the visuals to "come alive" as if the characters and objects in the film are actually in the same room as the patron.

02
Will the cinema screen be able to project the 3D effects successfully as the screen is flat?

Yes, with special 3D projection equipment & the special 3D glasses. A pair of 3D glasses will be made available to patrons that will help create the illusion of depth required to make the on-screen visuals "pop out" of the screen, as if it were right in front of you.

03
How often will the Cineplex screen 3D titles?

We are looking at showcasing several movies in 3D moving forward and the frequency of this would approximately be at least once every 2 months as we depend on film distributors to confirm if there is any upcoming release available in 3D format.

04
Are we able to know in advance which movies are going to be screened in 3D?

Yes, we will advertise this information online and within our cineplex premises as soon as we receive confirmation from the film distributors on the availability of the movie in 3D format.

05
Why is a 3D movie ticket more expensive than regular movie ticket?

Reel Cinemas has attached a premium value to 3D tickets because of the investment costs required to ensure a smooth and efficient presentation and experience for patrons. Unlike 2D digital presentations, the costs involved in the equipment, glasses, manpower and service / equipment maintenance are significantly higher. This is also a price model adapted from countries who already showcase 3D movies as a permanent feature i.e. USA, Australia.

06
Are complimentary movies passes allowed?

Yes, 3D complimentary passes or vouchers are accepted at Reel Cinemas as long as they are valid and mention 3D.

07
What happens if there is a defect found on the glasses before the movie?

You will need to approach the usher at the back of the hall to exchange your pair for another one before the start of the main feature.

08
What happens if there is a defect found on the glasses during the movie? (eg: I don't feel the 3D effect)

You will need to approach the usher at the back of the hall to exchange your pair for another one.

09
Are the 3D glasses free-sized?

Reel Cinemas at The Dubai Mall & Dubai Marina Mall offers adult and child size 3D glasses that patrons can choose from.

10
What should I do if I feel nauseous / ill?

Patrons who feel ill should remove their 3D glasses, close their eyes and relax. If you need further assistance, please leave the hall and approach any of our ushers stationed just outside the hall.

11
Am I able to change the movie if I feel like I'm not able to continue with the 3D effects?

We regret that no refund can be extended should the main feature commence punctually and proceed with no faults / interruptions.

12
Can I get a full refund if I find the 3D effects disturbing?

Reel Cinemas will address incidents like these on a case-by-case basis but the company policy would be to not facilitate a refund if there were no inherent faults in the presentation from a technical or service provision perspective as patrons are free to leave the hall should the 3D effects not sit well with them.

13
Why are children below 2 years of age not allowed into the hall?

Reel Cinemas is not insisting that children below 2 years of age not be allowed into the hall. We do not recommend it simply because, every patron admitted needs a pair of 3D glasses and to redeem a pair, the person needs an admission ticket. As a child below 2 years of age does not require a separate admission ticket, this would mean an additional ticket purchase on the part of the parent / guardian. As the glasses are a one-size-fits all design, it may not fit the child well, which may inevitably mar the viewing pleasure of the child. Nevertheless Reel Cinemas respects the decision made by parents / guardian but will need for the child to have a separate admission ticket purchased under such circumstances.

14
If there is no usher stationed outside hall during the movie, what should I do?

Please approach the usher station in front of the main entrance and he / she will assist you.

15
Can I purchase the 3D glasses as a memento?

Movie tickets purchased include a pair of 3D glasses and you may take them home as a memento.
